# Site Structure

## Proposed Sections

1. Hero: 13king, black-based photogenic key visual, short concept, Instagram contact action
2. Atmosphere: approachable shisha BAR near Osaka Metropolitan University/Nakamozu with a mature VIP feel
3. Features: karaoke, darts, shisha, VIP/private room
4. Owner Message: approachable greeting and DM invitation
5. Drink Menu: detailed menu examples, all-you-can-drink, shots separate
6. Pricing: charge, VIP/private room fee, all-you-can-drink, shots separate
7. Access: address, route from Osaka Metropolitan University, embedded Google Map
8. Hours and Contact: 22:00-last, Instagram contacts
9. Legal Notice: alcohol and age-related note

## Navigation

- Features
- Owner
- Menu

- Access
- Contact

## Copy Direction

- Tone: Nightlife, approachable, energetic, slightly mature, VIP-friendly, polished rather than AI-like.
- Japanese/English balance: Japanese first, short English accents where useful.
- Must-use words: 13king, 22:00-last, karaoke, darts, shisha, VIP room, all-you-can-drink.
- Avoid words: Student discount or youth-targeted language unless explicitly approved.

## Visual Direction

- Base: Black or near-black backgrounds.
- Accents: Gold, white, glass highlights, and black/white marble texture. Use Instagram's purple-based gradient for Instagram buttons only.
- Imagery: Interior, VIP room, drinks, shisha, karaoke/darts moments, exterior entrance if available.
- Layout feel: Mobile-first, bold first view, easy-to-scan pricing, clear Instagram actions.
- Avoid: Childlike colors, overly cheap discount-style visuals, cluttered flyer-like design, generic AI-looking neon/glow-heavy design.
