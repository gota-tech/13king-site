# Requirements

## Business Requirements

- Increase awareness among adult visitors near Osaka Metropolitan University.
- Make essential visit information easy to find on mobile.
- Give first-time visitors confidence about atmosphere, pricing, and access.
- Present 13king as approachable for the student-area nightlife scene while still feeling mature, stylish, and VIP-friendly.

## Content Requirements

- Bar name: 13king
- Short concept: Late-night bar near Osaka Metropolitan University with karaoke, darts, shisha, and VIP room.
- Hero image or visual asset
- Address: 〒591-8023 大阪府堺市北区中百舌鳥町6丁998-9
- Map link
- Embedded Google Map
- Owner greeting section
- Detailed drink menu section
- Opening hours: 22:00-last
- Pricing:
  - Charge: 500 yen
  - VIP/private room fee: 500 yen
  - All-you-can-drink: 1,500 yen
  - Shots: separate charge
- Features: Karaoke, darts, shisha, VIP/private room
- Reservation or contact method via Instagram
- Instagram links:
  - https://www.instagram.com/13king25c/
- Age/legal notice if alcohol is shown
- Brand mood: black-based marble, Instagrammable, slightly mature, VIP feel, approachable. Use Instagram's purple-based gradient only for Instagram actions.

## Functional Requirements

- Mobile-first responsive layout
- iPhone-first layout for common widths such as 375px, 390px, and 430px
- Fast first load
- Clear navigation to access, menu, and contact
- External links for map and social media
- Basic SEO metadata
- Clear price display on mobile
- Prominent Instagram contact buttons
- Embedded Google Map on the LP
- Detailed drink menu that can be updated later with official menu details

## Non-Functional Requirements

- Accessible contrast and readable type sizes
- Images optimized for web delivery
- No layout shift from dynamic content
- Works in current Safari, Chrome, and mobile browsers
- Tone and visuals should not read as targeting minors.
- Visual design should feel nightlife-oriented and photogenic without becoming visually cluttered.
- Black should be the dominant base color, balanced with readable text contrast and accent colors.
- Use `シーシャBAR` consistently across the LP.

## Acceptance Criteria

- A first-time adult visitor can identify the location, atmosphere, pricing, hours, and how to contact the bar within 30 seconds.
- The site is usable at mobile widths from 375px and desktop widths from 1280px.
- Lighthouse checks for accessibility and SEO do not reveal critical issues.
- Alcohol and shisha content includes an age-appropriate notice.
- The first viewport communicates `13king`, nightlife atmosphere, VIP/private-room availability, and Instagram contact clearly.
