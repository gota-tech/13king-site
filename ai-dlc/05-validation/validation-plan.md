# Validation Plan

## Visual QA

- Check mobile layout at 375px and 430px widths.
- Check desktop layout at 1280px and wider.
- Confirm no text overlaps, clipped buttons, or unreadable contrast.

## Accessibility

- Keyboard navigation works.
- Focus states are visible.
- Images have useful alt text.
- Color contrast is readable.

## Performance

- Images are compressed and appropriately sized.
- JavaScript is minimal.
- No avoidable render-blocking work.

## SEO

- Title and meta description exist.
- Open Graph tags exist.
- Local business information is crawlable.

## Legal and Content

- Alcohol-related content avoids underage targeting.
- Business hours, address, and contact details are accurate.
